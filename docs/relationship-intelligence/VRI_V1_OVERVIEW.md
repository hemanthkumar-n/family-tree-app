# Vamsha Relationship Intelligence (VRI) V1

## Purpose

VRI enables Vamsha to understand:

- Who a person is
- How they are related
- What the viewer calls them
- Language-specific relationship terms

## Supported Languages

- Telugu
- Tamil
- Kannada
- Malayalam
- Hindi
- Punjabi
- Marathi

## Core Concepts

Canonical Relationship

Examples:

- father
- mother
- fathers_elder_brother
- fathers_younger_brother
- mothers_brother
- co_sister
- co_brother

Language Mapping

Examples:

fathers_younger_brother

Telugu -> Babai

Hindi -> Chacha

Tamil -> Chithappa

Viewer Context

Same person may be:

- Bammardi
- Mama
- Uncle

depending on the viewer.
