# Vamsha Space Model

## Principle
Vamsha does not use tree-per-user architecture.

Vamsha uses collaborative FamilySpaces.

## Space Types
- Clan Space
- Branch Space
- Nuclear Family Space
- Custom Family Space
- Event Space
- Social Circle Space (future)

## Rules
- A person may belong to multiple spaces
- A user may administer multiple spaces
- Spaces are collaborative, not owned trees
- Marriage can connect spaces
- Event spaces may include non-family guests

- Natakam Clan
- Natakam Chennai Branch
- Ravi Nuclear Family
