# Vansha MVP Scope

## Objective
Deliver a privacy-aware living family relationship intelligence MVP.

## Included in MVP
### Authentication
- Google sign in
- profile onboarding

### Core Identity
- self profile creation
- person records
- profile claim workflow

### Family Management
- create family space
- add relatives manually
- relationship mapping
- parent support
- spouse support
- sibling support
- children support

### Privacy & Governance
- privacy controls
- branch access requests
- ownership workflows

### Graph Experience
- family tree visualization
- relationship exploration
- lazy branch expansion

### Invitations
- invite family members
- claim person profiles

## Excluded from MVP
- events
- social feed
- legacy vault
- AI intelligence
- monetization
